Lifeable

– 2021

Mobile design for Lifeable, an app to educate the youth on basic life skills.

Problem

Teens and young adults need a way to learn essential life skills not taught to them in educational institutions.

Solution

My team created a mobile app that provides young users with quick and easy lessons that teach essential life skills from a broad range of topics including finances, domestic skills, self-care, relationships, and career training.

My Role

UI Designer
UX Researcher


Time Frame

2 Weeks

Background Research Ideation Prototype
BACKGROUND
Blind Cat Rescue & Sanctuary

Blind Cat Rescue & Sanctuary is a lifetime sanctuary for blind, FIV positive and Leukemia positive cats located in St. Pauls, North Carolina. It was founded by a mother and daughter team to answer the need for a safe space for blind cats that were deemed not adoptable by regular shelters. Blind cats are regularly killed at shelters because of their condition, and this is the case for FIV+ and Leukeumia positive cats as well.

Website

Upon reviewing the Blind Cat website, our team discovered several major flaws. The biggest one was that the Donate button was difficult to find, which could surely hinder some potential donations. Our team needed to conduct a total overhaul.

RESEARCH
User Interviews

We conducted four user interviews with users who had donated to non-profits in the past. Our objective was to understand why users donate to non-profits and if they would donate again. We discovered that most of our interviewees had concerns about a non-profit's legitimacy.

Problem Statement

How might we redesign the Blind Cat Rescue & Sanctuary website to reach more users and raise awareness about cats with special needs?
